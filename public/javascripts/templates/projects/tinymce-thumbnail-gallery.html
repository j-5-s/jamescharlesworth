TinyMCE Thumbnail Gallery - WordPress Plugin

WordPress, PHP, Frontend JavaScript, Open Source, Design

WordPress is a wonderful platform to use as a blog or website CMS. One thing that makes it great is its wonderful plugin functionality. In creating many different WordPress sites I always found photo gallery plugins that tried to do too much. I decided to create a simple photo gallery plugin that did just what i wanted. It easily allows you to insert a gallery anywhere into your page in a clean fashion. It's been downloaded over 11k times and growing.
