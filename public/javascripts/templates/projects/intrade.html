Intrade - Node.js Module

jQuery, JavaScript, Open Source, UX

Intrade is a prediction market where you can buy or sell shares of future events. Yes, its a bit of a gamble, but I find it really interesting to get an idea of what the mass of people out there think is going to happen. I created a simple Node.js module that uses their api to fetch data on the markets.
