WestHost PHP Contest - SEO Campaign

PHP, Symfony, Frontend JavaScript, Design

SEO is not about link building or other tactics to garner "synthetic links". It's online marketing. Find a way to engage your target market and give them a reason to talk about you. The WestHost PHP contest was just that. I built a PHP contest on the Symfony framework allowing for users to submit code. It also allowed for anyone to vote on a submission using a bayesian algorithm to rank the results. Furthermore it had SEO friendly tags and SEO friendly urls.

The Gist

  • Generated authentic high Pagerank links
  • Powerful social media buzz
  • Engaged the target market
  • Created wonderful content from the community
