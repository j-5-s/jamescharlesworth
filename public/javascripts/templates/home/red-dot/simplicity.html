The red dot is simplicity

So far you are at least two clicks into this site, yet there was nothing but a dot to lead you here. A clean page with just a few choices. Enough to entice you, but not too much to overload your ability to navigate.

It's about understanding what your users are asking themselves while on the page and immediately providing the answers. It gets a bit more complex behind the scenes analyzing analytics and usability tests, but on the surface it's just plan simple.
