About Me

JavaScript

Working at The Home Depot I write validations on product data in a server side JavaScript environment. Working with product data can get complicated fast and JavaScript is a great language for it with its dynamic typing and freedom of expression.

I also have created a few Node.js apps that help make my job easier. Node.js is extremely fast and forces you to write small and simple applications, which in my opionion are the best kind.

As for frontend JavaScript, I stand on the shoulders of giants. I am constantly making rich applications with jQuery, Backbone.js, Underscore and require.js.

PHP

I've been writing PHP code for 7 years now. Starting off as a small business owner creating my own custom PHP applications I have come a long way. I am Zend PHP 5.0 certified and enjoy working with PHP mainly because of all the great open source frameworks like Symfony and WordPress.

I've built a majority of my applications and websites with PHP but am starting to lean towards Node.js for backend programming. I don't think there is a one size fit all approach though and consider all the variables.

SEO

I spent three years as an SEO making websites really popular and generating a strong ROI. I decided to drop SEO several years ago because I realized that the best SEO is a great product offering and not link building or other tactics used solely for ranking in the SERPs.

I found that the best SEO campaigns consisted of something tangible like great content or strong product knowledge that is well presented and an attitude to go along with it. The problem is that most SEO clients don't want to provide that and as an SEO it can't be done as it must come from within the client's business.

Design

I enjoy creating simple web designs and nothing too complex. I find that any design that makes the content standout is usually a good one. I hand code all my HTML templates and work with CSS frameworks such as Compass and SASS along with Blueprint.

I am the first to admit that design is not my strong suite so if you are looking for an amazing design i recommend you find someone else. My sites and applications focus on data driven applications with a solid user experience.
